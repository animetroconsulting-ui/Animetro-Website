# Animetro Brand Package Manifest

## Locked Identity Direction

This brand package preserves the approved Animetro visual identity. It is a refinement and production rebuild of the existing AI-rendered artwork, not a redesign.

Preserved elements:

- A infinity monogram
- Navy and gold luxury palette
- Oval academic seal
- Wreath institutional emblem
- Serif typography hierarchy
- Academic prestige and Ivy League advisory tone

Production improvements:

- Vector-style geometry
- Sharper typography
- Cleaner spacing
- Consistent navy and gold rendering
- Transparent PNG exports without glow or halo effects
- SVG-ready source assets
- Retina-ready web and social exports

## Do Not Change

- Do not replace the A infinity monogram.
- Do not remove the navy and gold luxury direction.
- Do not turn the brand into modern startup styling.
- Do not add metallic bevels, fake 3D, blur, glow, painterly texture, or AI-rendered shadows.
- Do not mix English and Chinese within body content.
